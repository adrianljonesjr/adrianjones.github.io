The Borgen Project

Adrian Jones

The Borgen Project is a non-profit organization that works to make poverty a focus in U.S. foreign policy. During my time at The Borgen Project I had the opportunity to assist in advocating the organization's mission to local congressional leaders through a series of media outreach efforts including curating social media posts, developing informational videos and sending weekly emails to congressional offices.

I also was fortunate to lead a successful fundraising campaign raising over $800 to help the Borgen Project continue in its fight in reducing poverty. In efforts to build awareness of global poverty issues, I was also able to contribute to Borgen Magazine, the in-house publication of the organization, through creating SEO friendly articles to increase the organizations online and community presence. Through my efforts, I was offered a position as a Regional Director and continue to be a part of the Borgen team!
